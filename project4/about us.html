ABOUT US

Hi Beauties!

Kokoa beauty is a black owned small business, and it specializes in providing a wide range of options when it comes to lip glosses and lipsticks. The shades of glosses offered by this business change with the seasons, ensuring that customers always have access to the latest colors. What sets Kokoa beauty apart is its commitment to inclusivity and accommodation for all individuals. Regardless of a person's background or characteristics, the website aims to offer products and services that can be enjoyed by everyone. Discrimination or exclusion based on any factors is strictly avoided, as the ultimate goal is to create a platform that caters to the diverse needs and desires of every customer

Ultimately, the main objective of the website is to create a seamless and enjoyable shopping experience for customers. Kokoa beauty aims to not only sell products but also build a loyal community of individuals who appreciate the value of high-quality lip glosses and lipsticks. The lip gloss website primarily focuses on individuals who have a deep passion for glosses and a general fondness for makeup. It goes beyond just selling products by providing a community section where users can find beauty tips and tutorials. Additionally, there is a customer review section where individuals can share their experiences and recommendations, fostering a sense of community and trust
